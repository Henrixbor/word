# Privacy Policy Template

This app is a multiplayer word game.

## Data we process

- Account identifiers you create in the app, such as username and player ID
- Gameplay data such as guesses, scores, rankings, invites, and match state
- Basic technical data needed to run the service, such as device and network diagnostics in logs

## Why we process it

- To run matchmaking and multiplayer gameplay
- To restore your profile and match progress
- To show rankings, friends, and team activity
- To diagnose crashes, abuse, and service issues

## Third parties

- Apple TestFlight / App Store Connect for iOS distribution
- Google Play for Android distribution
- Railway for backend hosting

## Game Center

If you choose to use Game Center features on iOS, the app may use your Game Center identity and, where permitted, your Game Center friends to help connect you with people you know in the game.
